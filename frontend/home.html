About Us


Manan - A Techno Surge, the computer technical society of JCBUST, YMCA is a community of computer and technology geeks a.k.a. the developers, designers, competitive coders and even gamers who aim to break the world of codes and walk a step ahead of the world by debugging issues throughout the year. Manan gives the right atmosphere to all the competitive coders and developers where they can have healthy discussions regarding various upcoming technologies, hacks, complex algorithms and much more. Manan also works to arrange sessions regularly which are usually open to all those wish to learn. We have had an awesome record in college placements where most of it's members made it to companies like Amazon and samsung with decent packages. Manan gives you a platform to try. Now its your responsibilty how hard you try.

MANTHAN is the place for those who wish to join us. It is our entrance test which gives all the juniors an opportunity to join this amazing computer society of JCBUST i.e. MANAN. Remember, it will be as hard and tough as hacking NASA with html but as easy as to write a "Hello World" programme, so come for the auditions and give ur best.


Audition Process




Round 1: Written Test
This round consists of a written test consisting of questions related to aptitude, coding and basic programming skills in any one of the technologies, web or android. It is compulsary for all those who wish to join us. (Note)


Round 2: Project Showcasing
This is a project show casing round where you will have to present us a project. This project can be anything varying from a simple piece of code to a full stack website.


Round 3: Personal Interview
This round will be the personal interview round to check your technical skills and confidence


NOTE:
1. Second Year students will have to appear for an online coding test during round 1.
2. There will be no shortlisting after first round i.e. everyone is eligible for project showcasing round.
3. The project showcasing is not mandatory but you would ne given preference too opt for it.
